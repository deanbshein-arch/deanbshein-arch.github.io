🍕 McCain — Frozen In Time
✕
File Work McCain Tropfest About
💡 Insight
Italians visit their Nonna's to share stories and learn about Italian cuisine. But Nonna's cooking methods have become a lost art as food delivery apps and the hustle of today's world have pushed aside her culinary traditions. Nonna isn't going to be around forever.
🧠 Idea
Preserve your Nonna's pizza recipe by releasing it as its own McCain's pizza.
⚙️ Execution
Tell us your Nonna's recipe via #FrozenInTime and we'll choose favourites to bring to life. Every recipe goes into McCain's online freezer — accessible for years to come.
